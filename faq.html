FAQ and Suggestions

What inspired this project?

This project was inspired by my interest in music and coding. For my bachelor thesis (Senior Project), I knew I wanted to build some sort of musical website or software. After some research, I landed on this idea, and from there I slowly started to build my theoretical and technical references and sources.

What are the next updates to this project?

My next goal for this project is to include some visualizations of the audio being played, either the frequency, or the pitch. That is still under development!
