This is my Senior Project thesis! My aim was to create a platform to play and experiment with microtonal music, accessible to all people and knowledge levels. I am relying on Tune.js (web audio tuning library of microtonal and just intonation scales by Andrew Bernstein and Ben Taylor, 2015). I have adapted their work into this website, full credit goes to them: Tune.js.

In the Play section, you can select your microtonal scale and play around with it!
In the History & Theory section you can find some information about microtonal music.
In the Samples & Tutorial section you can find tutorials on how to start and use this website, as well as some samples I made.
In the Scale list section, you can find a complete list of the available tunings and temperaments.
In the FAQ section, you can find the FAQ section and how to reach me for suggestions.
